Content Complaints – Hoichoi

ONLINE CURATED CONTENT (REGULATION AND COMPLAINT PROCESS)

Hoichoi has always focused on providing entertainment and path-breaking shows and concepts making viewing an enjoyable experience. We also believe that freedom of expression and creative license comes with the responsibility to adopt robust standards in the content we offer our viewers.

Consistent with this approach, we have proactively set up an internal monitoring body, the Content Regulation department, which monitors all content prior to publishing to ensure that it complies with applicable laws of the land and is also in keeping with globally accepted standards.

Filing a Complaint

If you have any complaints with respect to any Content or User Material(s), correspondence should be sent to the attention of Grievance Officer, Mrs. Sera Banerjee by emailing content-grievances@hoichoi.tv with the following details:

  • Name of the complainant
  • E-mail address of the complainant
  • User log-in ID of the complainant
  • Title of the Content (with URL)
  • Nature of complaint
  • If applicable, timestamps for the Content: [xx minute] – [xx minute]
  • Details of the basis of complaint
  • Requested relief

Every complaint should bear your signature, either digital or written. Hoichoi may request further information as needed to resolve your complaint.

Acknowledgement & Resolution

Hoichoi shall issue an acknowledgement within 24 hours of receiving the complaint and reply with a decision/action within 15 working days. Additional time may be needed if more information is required. Complaints may be withdrawn at any time; withdrawn or abandoned complaints are deemed disposed of.

Possible Actions

Upon examination of the complaint, Hoichoi may undertake one or multiple of the following measures:

  • Reclassify age rating of relevant content
  • Include a warning card or disclaimer in content descriptors
  • Edit synopsis of relevant content

For any doubts about content regulations or the complaint process, contact support@hoichoi.tv.
