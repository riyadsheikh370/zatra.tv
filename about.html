About Us

Zatra is an on-demand video streaming platform for Bengalis worldwide, offering an extensive collection of 600+ Bengali movies, 150+ original web series, shorts and documentaries, with new content being added every week. We also have Hindi dubbed versions of all our original shows and our content is available with English subtitles.

Whether you are from Bengal or not, whether you live in Bengal or not… with Zatra, you can enjoy the Best in Bengali Entertainment from your device… anytime, anywhere!
